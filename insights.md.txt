 Key Insights & Recommendations

# Growth
- Orders and revenue show month-over-month variation with seasonal patterns.

# Products
- A small subset of categories drives the majority of revenue.
- Several products show high order volume but low revenue, indicating low-price items.

# Customers
- Most customers make a single purchase.
- A small loyal segment contributes repeat revenue.

# Operations
- Delayed deliveries account for a higher share of low review scores.
- Average review scores mask dissatisfaction caused by late delivery.

# Recommendations
- Improve delivery SLAs for high-value orders
- Retarget one-time customers
- Introduce loyalty programs for repeat buyers
- Monitor delivery delays as an early churn indicator
